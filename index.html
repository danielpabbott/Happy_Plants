Why?

We are taught a lot of great things during our education, but not all of them are applicable on a basic human needs level. Raising plants is an easy, cheap, and very valuable skillset that gets overlooked in traditional education.

What?

We have developed a system pairing app based grow instruction with real life gardening. After tutorial completion the customer will receive a pot and seeds to start their very own home garden! The best part is the system can be used in any environment from urban to rural.
